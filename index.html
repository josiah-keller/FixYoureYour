Fix You're Your

Fix You're Your is a Twitter bot that "corrects" random peoples' use of the words your and you're. It is meant purely as a joke, and deliberately gets things wrong sometimes.

If you reply to the bot, it may reply to you a second time. However, aside from its initial tweet to you and the potential second reply, it should not keep replying to you. If it does, please reach out by DM to let us know and feel free to block or mute the account.
